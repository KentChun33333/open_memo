### Asset Allocation Strategy

| Asset Class | Allocation | Purpose | Expected Return |
|-------------|------------|---------|-----------------|
| Low-Risk (Bonds, Fixed Income) | 30% | Capital preservation | 3-5% p.a. |
| Equities (Index Funds, Stocks) | 50% | Growth | 8-12% p.a. |
| Venture Capital / Startups | 20% | High growth | 15-30%+ p.a. |
| Physical Assets (Real Estate, Gold) | 10-15% | Inflation hedge | 5-10% p.a. |
| SaaS Business | Variable | Passive income | 20-50%+ p.a. |

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
- Build emergency fund (6 months expenses)
- Pay off high-interest debt
- Set up automated investments
- Establish financial tracking system

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-24)
- Maximize retirement contributions
- Diversify equity portfolio
- Allocate to dividend-paying stocks
- Start side business (SaaS focus)

### Phase 3: Acceleration (Months 25-60)
- Reinvest dividends
- Increase VC allocation
- Invest in rental property
- Scale SaaS business

### Key Metrics to Track
- Net worth growth rate (target: 20-30% p.a.)
- Passive income coverage (target: 50% of expenses)
- Debt-to-asset ratio (target: <20%)

### Singapore-Specific Opportunities
- CPF OA/SA optimization
- S-REITs for income
- Local startup ecosystem for VC
- Property investment in Singapore/Malaysia
